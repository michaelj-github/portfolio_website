About me

A full stack web developer and sometime hiker

If I'm not responding to emails and text messages, I may be on a trail somewhere in the mountains. I'll be back soon!

Back when I was working on my undergraduate degree, I had an opportunity to help a small business with their accounting system. I met their tax accountant, and he introduced me to some of his other clients who also needed help with their accounting systems. By the time I finished school, I had my own consulting business, which of course I called Michael J Murphy Consulting.

A lot has happened since then, and along the way I’ve worked on some interesting projects for large and small companies.

While the pandemic was raging, I was glad to be able to work from home helping Zillow in their home buying business. When that came to an end, I had an opportunity to take some courses to update my full stack software engineering skill set. Now I’m available for consulting engagements and open to employment opportunities on-site or remote.

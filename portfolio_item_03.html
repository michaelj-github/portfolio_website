Project title: Find GARP

a website for investors

Find GARP is a website for investors to help with identifying companies with growing revenues and earnings.

Find GARP

This is another one of the projects I did for the Springboard Software Engineering program. It includes a React front end with a NodeJS/Express backend, and a PostgreSQL database. The backend provides an API for the frontend and the database is maintained via the finnhub.io API

Growth at a reasonable price (GARP) is a popular method used by investors to find companies to invest in. Investors like to buy stocks they believe they will be able to sell at a higher price at some point in the future.

Share prices are determined by Earnings Per Share and the Price/Earnings Ratio. The best companies for GARP investors are growing their revenues and earnings and can be purchased when their Price/Earnings ratios are not too high. Hence the name: Growth at a Reasonable Price.

Find GARP is a tool for identifying companies with growing revenues and earnings. When you log in you will see a list of growing companies. You can review some important financial details about the company and enter your own estimates for future growth rates and P/E ratios as well as some notes to explain your analysis..
